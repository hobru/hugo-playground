## Introduction

In Episode 65 of the SAP on Azure Video Podcast we talk about a new SAP customer engagement initiative on integrating SAP events with Microsoft events, talk about Microsoft Digitals approach to use a scream test to silence its unused servers, Private link with principal propagation, GitHub Universe 2021, vscode.dev, Azure Tips & Tricks, the Cloud Skill Challenge during Ignite, the very first Azure Friday episode, successful customers in the SAP on Azure Talk podcast #22 and episode 400 of the Azure Podcast. Then we listen to Denny Huang who joins us from Singapore to talk about his experience being a SAP Global Black Belt in the Asia region.
