## Introduction

In Episode #42 we talk about Power Platform and the connections to SAP, including the private preview for the new OData connector, Azure Sentinel SAP threat monitoring solution, Open Documentation Initiative at SAP, Free Student Offering for the SAP Learning Hub, SAPPHIRE Sessions and then take a closer look with Robert and Petra on the Azure DevOps Demo Generator.
