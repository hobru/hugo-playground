## Introduction

In Episode #45 of the SAP on Azure video podcast we talk about SAPPHIRE NOW! The Keynote with Thomas Saueressig and Scott Guthrie, other SAPPHIRE sessions, Coffee Corner Radio interview with Holger Müller and Martin Fischer, SAP Developer Insight Survey, Second Joint Hackathon from SAP & Microsoft, Why customers choose Azure, Build, Inspire and lots of "What the Hack" content. 
Then Ross Sponholtz talks about Azure Monitor for SAP focusing on HA Cluster monitoring.
