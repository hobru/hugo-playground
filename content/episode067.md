## Introduction

In Episode 67 of the SAP on Azure Video Podcast we talk about a new documentation for Azure Active Directory and SAP, resizing disks, a new upcoming virtual datacenter tour, the openSAP course for SAP + Microsoft, a hackathon for SAP & Power Platform and an upcoming webinar on SAP Solutions and Power Platform. Then Amber Singh from Microsoft Digital joins us to talk about End to End telemetry for SAP on Azure.
